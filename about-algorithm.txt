1. What is an algorithm?
Ans: An algorithm is a set of well-defined instructions to solve a particular problem

2. What are the characteristics of an algorithm?
Ans: Following are the characteristics:
i. Well defined inputs and outputs.
ii. Each step should be clear and unambiguous.
iii. Language independent

3. Why algorithms?
Ans: Learning algorithms makes a developer highly efficient at solving real-world application problems and make the process efficient.
